    Orange County IEEE for Young Professionals

    Project maintained by OC-IEEE-YP Hosted on GitHub Pages — Thanks to mattgraham

    Since 1884, the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE, pronounced “Eye-triple-E”) is the largest professional society in the world dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through IEEE’s highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. Despite its original charter as the Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers, the organization’s scope of interest has expanded into a range of diverse fields.

    Here at Orange County IEEE for Young Professionals, our IEEE Branch is headed by Irvin Huang and David Huang. Today, most of our members work in the professional world and continue their education beyond the bachelor degree.
